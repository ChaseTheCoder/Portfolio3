About

My career in a timeline to undertand what led me to software engineering.

  • 2012-2014

    Teach For America

    I accepted my TFA offer to teach early childhood education in Chicago. During my time I was able to teach children to write their names, key literacy and math skills, and support families.

  • 2014-Present

    Teaching

    I continued teaching in NYC and then back in Chicago. My data continously grew stronger. My first year with 2nd grade taking a state test showed one and half years worth of academic growth. This was no small task and took much effort as they were well below grade level when they began the year. When I'm passionate about my work I get it done.

  • 2019

    Idea of Software Engineering Sparked

    Hearing me talk about my passion for the benifits of tech in the classroom suggested I do a coding bootcamp. I wanted a career with upward mobility, but educational administration was not my passion.
